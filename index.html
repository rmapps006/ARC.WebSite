Our Legacy

A Legacy Forged in Copper

For over six decades, Abdul Rashid Copperware has been shaping handmade copper pieces in the heart of Lahore’s Walled City. Every hammer mark is a record of tradition, patience, and craftsmanship carried across three generations.

We do not manufacture. We make — slowly, by hand, the way our grandfather did in 1966.

The Craft

Made by Hands, Perfected by Time

01

Copper Selection

We hand-pick the purest sheet copper, judged by weight, tone and grain.

02

Shaping

The sheet is cut and coaxed into form over wooden and iron stakes.

03

Hand Hammering

Thousands of measured strokes give each piece its living, dimpled skin.

04

Polishing

Layer by layer the surface is brought to a warm, mirror-deep glow.

05

Final Finishing

Handles set, interiors tinned, every edge checked by a master’s eye.
